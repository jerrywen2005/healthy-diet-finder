Welcome to Healthy Diet Finder!

What We Do

Healthy Diet Finder helps you eat smarter by combining AI, nutrition data, and personalized planning tools. Whether you're meal-prepping, dining out, or optimizing for fitness goals — we’ve got your back.

How It Works

  1. Set your dietary goals, upload your DNA/lab info
  2. Let AI create a personalized plan for you
  3. Shop smart or find restaurants nearby
  4. Plan quicker using My Smart Planner

Key Features

🍽️ Restaurant Advisor: Find healthy places to eat nearby based on your goals
🛒 Grocery Planner: Smart grocery lists and meal plans built for your budget and diet
🧬 DNA Analysis: Upload DNA or lab results for tailored diet and workout plans
🤖 AI-Powered: Smart suggestions, tips, and coaching using OpenAI

Who It’s For

  • Fitness Enthusiasts – Looking for healthy places to eat out without compromising dietary goals
  • Meal Preppers – Want to save time and money by generating smart grocery lists and weekly meal plans
  • People with Specific Health Needs – Need personalized diet and fitness recommendations based on DNA, lab results, or medical conditions
  • Data-Driven Eaters – Like to track, review, and optimize their nutrition choices over time
  • Privacy-Conscious Users – Prefer secure logins and personalized spaces to manage their goals and data
